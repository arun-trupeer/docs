In this document, you will learn how to create professional intro and outro slides using the Trupeer platform. Each step will guide you through selecting visuals, adding images, and customizing text to enhance your slide presentations.

### Step 1: Access the Visuals Menu

Click on Visuals option.

![Screenshot 1](images/intro-outro/image1.jpg)

### Step 2: Navigate to Intro Slide Settings

From there, select the **Intro Slide** option.

![Screenshot 2](images/intro-outro/image2.jpg)

### Step 3: Enable Intro Slide Feature

Then enable the **intro slide**.

![Screenshot 3](images/intro-outro/image3.jpg)

### Step 4: Select Intro Slide Background

Then select intro slide image for the list of images.

![Screenshot 4](images/intro-outro/image4.jpg)

### Step 5: Upload Custom Images

You also have the option to **upload a new image** from the menu.

![Screenshot 5](images/intro-outro/image5.jpg)

### Step 6: Customize Intro Text

You can also edit the text in the Intro slides.

![Screenshot 6](images/intro-outro/image6.jpg)

### Step 7: Adjust Font Size

Adjust the font size by dragging or clicking on the size option.

![Screenshot 7](images/intro-outro/image7.jpg)

### Step 8: Change Font Color

Modify the font color by selecting the font color option.

![Screenshot 8](images/intro-outro/image8.jpg)

### Step 9: Set Slide Duration

Adjust the slide duration by dragging the duration slider to your desired length.

![Screenshot 9](images/intro-outro/image9.jpg)

### Step 10: Access Outro Settings

Next, click on the Outro Slide option.

![Screenshot 10](images/intro-outro/image10.jpg)

### Step 11: Enable Outro Slide

Then enable, **Add outro slide** option.

![Screenshot 11](images/intro-outro/image11.jpg)

### Step 12: Choose Outro Background

Then **select the image** from the list of images

![Screenshot 12](images/intro-outro/image12.jpg)

### Step 13: Preview Outro Slide

Finally, now you can see the **outro slide.**

![Screenshot 13](images/intro-outro/image13.jpg)
